Our Solution To Stop World Hunger

Free School Meals

Our solution to end world hunger is free school meals. We want global contribution to help struggling countries incorporate free meals at their schools. The idea is to create a global fund where every country contributes a fair share. The fund is then controlled by a designated team. Each nation chooses one representative to represent their country and the team then delegates the fund. By providing free school meals in these countries, it would create a better environment for these children. Instead of having to work to feed themselves, it would prevent them from dropping out of school and it would be giving them a better opportunity of getting an education.
